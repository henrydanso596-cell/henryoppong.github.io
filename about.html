Computer security refers to the protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft, or damage.

Computer Security Awareness helps:

Protect personal info: use strong passwords, 2FA.

Be cautious online: phishing, suspicious links, downloads.

Keep software updated: OS, apps, antivirus.

Use backups: regular data backups.

Monitor accounts: detect suspicious activity.

Use secure connections: HTTPS, VPNs.

Beware of social engineering: human psychology exploits.
